About the Rexplorer

The Rexplorer helps fans of the Rex Factor podcast explore the scores awarded to the various Monarchs and Consorts.

The card images are all sourced from Wikimedia Commons apart from some of the early English monarch images which come from the Genealogical Chronicle of the English Kings from the British Library (shelfmark Royal MS 14 B VI).
